常见问题

如何恢复购买？

如果你已经使用当前 Apple ID 购买过专业版，请在 App 的付费页点击“恢复购买”。如果仍无法恢复，请确认当前设备登录的是购买时使用的 Apple ID。

如何管理或取消订阅？

月度和年度专业版订阅可以在 iOS 的 Apple ID 订阅管理中变更或取消。终身专业版为一次性购买，不会自动续费。

照片和报告保存在哪里？

项目、照片和报告默认保存在当前设备。需要备份时，请先导出 PDF 报告或带水印照片。

为什么照片没有地址或 GPS？

请确认你已经允许定位权限，并且设备能够获取定位信息。室内、地下室、弱网络或系统定位服务关闭时，地址和 GPS 可能不可用或不准确。

如何删除数据？

你可以在 App 内删除项目、照片和报告。删除 App 通常也会删除本地数据，除非你已经导出副本或系统保留了设备备份。

FAQ

How do I restore purchases?

If you already purchased Pro access with the current Apple ID, open the paywall in the app and tap Restore Purchases. If restore still fails, confirm that the device is signed in with the Apple ID used for the purchase.

How do I manage or cancel a subscription?

Monthly and yearly Pro subscriptions can be changed or canceled in iOS Apple ID subscription settings. Lifetime Pro is a one-time purchase and does not renew.

Where are photos and reports stored?

Projects, photos, and reports are stored on the current device by default. Export PDF reports or stamped photos if you need backups.

Why is address or GPS missing?

Make sure location permission is allowed and the device can obtain location data. Indoors, underground areas, weak network conditions, or disabled system location services may make address and GPS unavailable or inaccurate.

How do I delete data?

You can delete projects, photos, and reports in the app. Removing the app usually removes local data unless you exported copies or the system preserved a device backup.
