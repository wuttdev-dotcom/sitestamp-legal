Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2026

SiteStamp Camera helps you take work photos with time, address, GPS, project name, and notes, and manage projects, photos, and PDF reports on your device. This policy explains how the app handles data.

Information We Process

  • Project information, including project name, client name, address, notes, and photo stages.
  • Photo information, including photos taken in the app, photo notes, tags, and stamped photos.
  • Location information, when you grant permission, to add GPS coordinates and address details to new photos.
  • Report files generated as PDFs in the app.
  • Purchase status read through Apple StoreKit for subscriptions and in-app purchases.

Where Data Is Stored

SiteStamp Camera primarily stores projects, photos, reports, and settings on your current device. The app does not require an account and does not provide a custom cloud sync service.

Permissions

  • Camera access is used to take worksite photos.
  • Location access is used to add address and GPS watermarks when you allow it.
  • Photo Library access is used only when you choose to export or save stamped photos.

Purchases

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive your Apple ID password, card number, or payment credentials.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. You may choose to export or share photos and PDF reports through system sharing features. After export, handling by recipients is outside the app's control.

Retention and Deletion

You can delete projects, photos, and reports in the app at any time. Removing the app usually removes local app data unless you exported copies or the system preserved a device backup.

Children's Privacy

SiteStamp Camera is intended for work scenarios such as construction, inspection, and project records. It is not directed to children.

Changes

We may update this policy as product features or legal requirements change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
